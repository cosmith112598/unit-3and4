Each of our career specialists have years of experience in their fields. It is because of this experience we can provide you with tips and tricks that only years of experience can teach you. This is super helpful for those just starting out.

What is our end goals?

  1. Have you confident in you abilities!
  2. Make you comfortable scheduling and maintaining your scheduling!
  3. Help you decide on help needed for shoots (makeup, posing, hair, etc).
  4. Help you be the best you can be given your schedule and needs before shooting and help you plan for it.
  5. Help you find your niche in the industry so you can continue to grow. (glamour, pinup, urban, etc)

What if I have no clue what I am doing?

Don't worry! We all have been new to a sport or skill at one point in time. Our leaders are equipped to deal with the tasks that can seem overwhelming at first and teach you skills on how to manage them better. Pretty soon you'll be running the place when you get in with our professionals and let them mentor you. The first year is the hardest, but it doesn't have to be.


Do you work with my Agency?

To answer that you will need to look over your contract you were given or ask your agent in charge. We are happy to help you figure it out. We are always happy to take some of the stresses off of you. There is usually a clause that will go over their policy for Outside Help or posing coaches from different agencies. If you have any questions on your contract call your agency.
